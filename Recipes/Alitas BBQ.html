Alitas BBQ

BBQ chicken wings are a classic of American cuisine, a delicacy that never goes out of style and is always well received on any occasion. This recipe combines the crunch of chicken with the smoky sweetness of barbecue sauce, creating a feast of flavor that will delight all diners. Get ready to impress your guests with this easy and delicious recipe!

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 1 hour
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 30 minutes

Instructions

  1. Wash the chicken wings under cold water and dry them well with kitchen paper.
  2. In a large bowl, season the wings with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Make sure you coat all the pieces well with the spices.
  3. In another bowl, mix the barbecue sauce, honey, soy sauce and olive oil. Combine the ingredients well until you obtain a homogeneous mixture and set aside.
  4. Pour half of the prepared BBQ sauce over the seasoned chicken wings. Make sure to cover all the pieces with the sauce.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour. If you have more time, marinating overnight will intensify the flavor.
  6. Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  7. Place the chicken wings on a baking sheet previously greased or lined with baking paper.
  8. Bake the wings for 25-30 minutes, turning halfway through, or until golden brown and crispy.
  9. While the wings are baking, heat the rest of the BBQ sauce in a saucepan over medium heat for a few minutes, stirring occasionally.
  10. Once the wings are ready, serve them hot with the hot BBQ sauce for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken wings
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce (you can use store-bought or make homemade)
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
