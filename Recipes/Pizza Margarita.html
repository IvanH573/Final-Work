Pizza Margarita

Margherita pizza is an icon of Italian cuisine, appreciated for its simplicity and explosion of fresh flavors. This traditional recipe combines basic but tasty ingredients to create a culinary masterpiece that delights all palates. Follow these steps to create your own Margherita pizza in the comfort of your home.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 40 minutes
  • Preparation: 35 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats so it warms up as well.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface until you get the desired thickness. If you prefer a thinner, crispier base, stretch it further. For a thicker, fluffier base, leave it a little thicker.
  3. Transfer the stretched dough to a baking sheet or pizza peel if you are using a pizza stone.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around it.
  5. Place the mozzarella slices on top of the tomato sauce, making sure to cover the entire surface of the pizza.
  6. Distribute the tomato slices over the mozzarella.
  7. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste on the pizza.
  8. Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and crispy, and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
  9. Once ready, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before cutting it.
  10. Before serving, scatter the fresh basil leaves over the pizza and drizzle with a generous drizzle of extra virgin olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough (you can make it at home or buy it at a store)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce 200g fresh
  • mozzarella, cut into thin slices
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Nutrition

Calories 250-300kcal per portion
Carbs 30-40g
Protein 10-15g
Fat 10-15g
