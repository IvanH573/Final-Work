Sushi

Immerse yourself in Japanese culinary art with this exquisite salmon and avocado sushi recipe. With a perfect combination of freshness, texture and flavor, this homemade sushi will transport you to the bustling streets of Tokyo. Follow these simple steps and get ready to impress your guests with an unforgettable dining experience.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 2 hours
  • Preparation: 35 minutes
  • Cooking: 25 minutes

Instructions

  1. Rinse 2 cups of sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook the rice according to package directions and let it cool to room temperature.
  3. Once cooled, season the rice with 1/4 cup seasoned rice vinegar and mix gently to combine.
  4. Place a sheet of nori seaweed on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down.
  5. Spread a thin, even layer of rice over the nori, leaving an edge of about 1cm uncovered.
  6. Place strips of salmon, avocado and cucumber in the center of the rice.
  7. With the help of the bamboo mat, roll the sushi firmly but carefully, making sure the ingredients are well sealed.
  8. Using a sharp knife, cut the sushi roll into slices about 2.5cm thick.
  9. Repeat the process with the rest of the ingredients.
  10. Place the sushi slices on a serving plate and decorate with toasted sesame seeds.
  11. Serve the sushi accompanied by soy sauce and wasabi to taste.
  12. Enjoy this delicious salmon and avocado sushi creation with friends and family, savoring every fresh and flavorful bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sushi rice
  • 4 sheets of nori seaweed
  • 200g fresh salmon, cut into thin strips
  • 1 ripe avocado, cut into thin slices
  • 1 cucumber, cut into thin strips
  • Soy sauce, to accompany
  • Wasabi, to serve
  • toasted sesame, to decorate

Nutrition

Calories 200-400Kcal
Carbs 30-40g
Protein 5-10g
Fat 5-10g
