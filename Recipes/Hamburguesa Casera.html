Hamburguesa Casera

Homemade hamburgers are a true delight that transports us to moments of comfort and flavor. Preparing them at home not only ensures the freshness of the ingredients, but also allows you to customize every aspect, from the choice of meat to the accompaniments. Here is a basic recipe to create homemade burgers that will surely become a hit on your table.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, onion, garlic, egg, breadcrumbs, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Mix all the ingredients well until they are completely integrated.
  2. Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape each portion into a ball. Then, flatten each ball to form burgers of the desired size and thickness. Make sure they are of a uniform thickness so they cook evenly.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add a little oil or butter. When hot, place the burgers in the pan. Cook each side for about 4-5 minutes, or until golden brown on the outside and cooked through on the inside. If you prefer, you can also cook them on the grill.
  4. While the burgers are cooking, you can lightly toast the burger buns in a skillet or in the oven.
  5. When the burgers are ready, remove them from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes. Meanwhile, prepare the ingredients to assemble the burgers: lettuce leaves, tomato slices, onion rings, cheese, sauce and any other accompaniment you want.
  6. To assemble the burgers, place a cooked hamburger patty on the bottom half of a hamburger bun. Add the tomato slices, lettuce, onion and any other ingredients you have chosen. Cover with the top half of the bread.
  7. And ready! Serve your homemade burgers hot and accompanied by your favorite garnishes.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of ground beef (you can use a mixture of beef and pork, or just beef depending on your preference)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed or finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves, tomato slices, onion rings (optional)
  • Cheddar cheese or your favorite cheese (optional)
  • Sauce of your choice (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, etc.)

Nutrition

Calories 250-400Kcal
Carbs 20-40g
Protein 15-30g
Fat 10-25g
